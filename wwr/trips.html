Trip Information

Our company does group trips at the Great Falls Dam Monday through Saturday each week, starting at 7:30 in the morning and lasting until 2:30pm. If there is a day that is considered dangerous due to weather, we will contact you and help set up another time! We will NOT allow any child under the age of 12 to go with us, and any other child above that age must know how to swim to join.

Pricing

We charge depending on the day of the week.
Monday - Friday = $100 an adult and $75 per child.
Saturday = $130 per adult and $100 per child.

Adult equipment = $40 per day
Child equipment = $25 per day
