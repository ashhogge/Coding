About Us

Welcome to Dry Oar Rafting Co.! We are your ultimate destination for thrilling white water rafting experiences. Our passionate team of river experts ensures safe, exciting adventures for everyone. With over a decade of sharing the joy of rapids, we cater to first-timers and seasoned rafters alike. Join us now to make a splash and ride the rapids through stunning river landscapes! Let's dive in together!

History

In 2008, a group of nature enthusiasts and thrill-seekers founded Dry Oar Rafting Co. with a single raft and a dream to share the joy of white water rafting. Over the years, they became known for delivering unforgettable experiences and advocating for river conservation. Today, with more than a decade of adventure under their belts, Dry Oar Rafting Co. continues to inspire people to embrace the spirit of exploration and immerse themselves in the beauty of untamed rivers. Join their river family and ride the rapids for an experience of a lifetime!
